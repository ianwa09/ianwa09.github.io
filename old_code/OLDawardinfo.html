Official Award Descriptions

2024 Inspire Award, 2nd Place: Most prestigious award, guarantees state qualification. The nominees are strong contendors for several award categories. NOTE: Due to the prestigious nature of the Inspire Award, nominees are unable to win 1st place for other awards, and can at most earn 2nd place.

2024 Winning Alliance, First Selection: The Winning Alliance wins the playoffs portion of qualifiers and each of the three teams automatically advances to state.

2024 Think Award, 2nd Place: Solving problems with critical thinking, and underlying mathematical concepts used in robot design.

2024 Connect Award, 2nd Place: Connecting with local STEM community and helping others understand FIRST and FTC.

2024 Control Award, 2nd Place: Using sensors and software to improve robot's functionality, such as autonomous operation and intelligent control.

2024 Innovate Award, 2nd Place: Innovating creative designs using the design process and making them a reality with tools like CAD.

2023 Motivate Award, 2nd Place: Community service award for outstanding service in local neighborhoods.

2022 Think Award, 3rd Place: See previous.
